Campaigns, for example...

Art Basel - Untitled

Official agency of record for Art Basel's newest fair, Untitled - the prestigious exhibit which takes place on Miami's South Beach. Untitled is the latest addition to Art Basel and has already been lauded by the art scene, The New York Times, Vogue and Art in America.

Driving sponsorships and event activation, Untitled in its second year at the distinguished annual art fair, will execute a series of bespoke events for brands, including Maserati.

Nike - Air Force II Launch

For the launch of Air Force II, a partnership with hip-hop mogul P Diddy was forged to create a dynamic art exhibit showcasing the new sneakers. Leveraging a network of influencers from the world of music, fashion, art and sport, the launch event encouraged participants to design the facade of their own sneakers as part of a creative collective and moment in time. The art exhibit, which was hosted in Chelsea, NY spearheaded the creative movement of the new sneaker while creating buzz and word-of-mouth.

Diesel - Rock n’ Roll Circus

To celebrate their 30th anniversary, a Rock n' Roll circus was devised and executed in Brooklyn, New York. The innovative circus-themed event was curated with an array of performers from stilt walkers to aerial acrobats and replete with fairground entertainment, including popcorn, cotton candy and authentic hotdog stands.

A first-of-its-kind musical performance with Hot Chip vs. Chaka Kahn and MIA vs. N.E.R.D. elevated the show-stopping experience to create one of the hottest events of the year.

Bungalow 8 New York - Documentary Screening

Created exclusive documentary screening series with Amy Sacco and B8 / Bungalow 8 in New York. To champion and provide a platform for discussion on societal issues traversing continents around the world, a collaboration with Snag Films - one of the leading film distributors in the U.S., has been forged to show a series of powerful, thought-provoking documentaries. The screening series debuted in the Fall 2013 at a variety of venues in NY and Brooklyn, including B8 - host venue, and Norwood House.

tribeca film festival - “savages” premiere

Devised bespoke art event for the premiere of “Savages” starring Blake Lively and John Travolta.

The after show premiere party showcased interpretations of characters from the film in savage, animal portrayals at The Tribeca Grand Hotel.

Event curation, sponsorships, guest list management were additionally handled for the VIP, influencer and consumer event.
